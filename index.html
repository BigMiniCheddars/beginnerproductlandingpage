Welcome to Security Solutions

Security Solutions is a Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) solution that provides insight into machine-generated data gathered from your IT environment. It's designed to deliver comprehensive visibility into high-priority threats, set alerts based on behaviors and anomalies, and automate the workflows for incident response.

Product Overview

  • Comprehensive Visibility: Splunk ES can ingest, normalize, and analyze vast amounts of data from various sources, offering a data-powered security solution. With the use of assistive AI, it provides insights into activities across your environment.
  • Accurate Detection with Context: The platform uses Risk-Based Alerting (RBA) to minimize false positives and focus on the most significant threats by scoring them according to risk.
  • Operational Efficiency: By coupling SIEM with Security Orchestration, Automation, and Response (SOAR) capabilities, Splunk ES streamlines the threat detection, investigation, and response process.

In terms of features, Security Solutions includes:

  • Analytics: It allows for the monitoring, detection, and investigation of threats with accuracy and speed.
  • Curated Detections: There are over 1,500 out-of-the-box detections provided by the Splunk Threat Research Team, aligned with industry frameworks.
  • Risk-based Alerting: This helps attribute risk scores to entities and alerts you when a certain threshold is exceeded, tackling the issue of alert fatigue.
  • Unifying Threat Detection, Investigation, and Response: Integrates workflows for a seamless transition from detection through to the response phase.
  • Visibility: The platform can handle data from all sources, ensuring nothing slips through the cracks and providing a complete view of your security posture.
